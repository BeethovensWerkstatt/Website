Am 18. Oktober 2017 war Federica Rovelli zum Mini-Symposium „Latest Research Shines New Light on Beethoven's Fifth and Eighth Symphonies“ der Boston University (Center for Beethoven Research) eingeladen worden und stellte das Referat „The Eighth is Singular. Some Hypothesis on the Genesis of Op. 93“ vor.
